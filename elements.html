Purpose of this website

“Sharing IT Know-How” is a cutting-edge educational effort created to encourage knowledge sharing within the community of first-year IT students. The purpose of this website is to improve the learning experience and develop a culture of collaboration and knowledge sharing among those who are just starting out in the field of Information Technology. “Sharing IT Know-How” recognizes the value of peer collaboration in IT education by creating a forum where students can draw on each other’s experiences and expertise to achieve academic and professional goals. This website enhances the educational experience while simultaneously preparing students for a successful future in the dynamic field of Information Technology.
